## Failure analysis

Two dimensions have non-zero PARTIAL counts:

**D5 (Anti-pattern) — 2 PARTIALs** (S10, S13)
- Pattern: SKILL.md's anti-pattern check is well-spec'd for single-Intake-then-Draft, but doesn't explicitly cover anti-patterns that arise from **state changes** during Polish or **topic pivots** during Intake.
- S10: Topic pivot to "should we sunset the product" is a higher-stakes pre-flight question than the original; SKILL.md doesn't say to re-run the pre-flight gate when the topic fundamentally shifts.
- S13: Adding a stats-wanting stakeholder should trigger sample-size recalculation; SKILL.md mentions re-running the evidence axis but not explicitly the sample-size step.

**D9 (Chat affordance) — 2 PARTIALs** (S10, S12)
- S10: SKILL.md handles Polish-amendments but not mid-Intake-topic-pivot.
- S12: SKILL.md's example "I need to know what decision this informs" phrasing assumes the gap is *What*; needs to generalize to any foundational gap.

**D2 (Foundational fields) — 1 PARTIAL** (S02)
- S02's image-attachment case: skill needs to ask Stakeholders + Research subjects + the actual decision after reading the image. SKILL.md covers this implicitly but doesn't have a worked example for "image-only-with-no-text" inputs.
